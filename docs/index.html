Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on E-Shop?

To create an account, click on the 'Register' button at the top right of the page. Fill in your details and follow the prompts to set up your account. Once registered, you can manage your cart and favorites more easily.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment gateways. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order's status on our shipping partner's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer support team to initiate a return. Ensure the items are in their original condition.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the 'Contact Us' page on our website. We are available via email, phone, or live chat to assist you with any queries or issues.
